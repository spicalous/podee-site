Features

Take orders on any device

Podee supports all device sizes. Take orders from your phone, tablet or a desktop computer.

Store and search customer data

Reduce errors and speed up placing orders. Customers who have ordered before will have their information saved so you don't need to ask again.

Analyse and improve business performance

Easily view your past business performance or your popular items ordered.

Print orders and receipts

Print receipts for your customers, for your kitchen staff, and for your delivery drivers.
